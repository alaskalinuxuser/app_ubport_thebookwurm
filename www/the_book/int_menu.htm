  • "Rightly Dividing the Word of Truth"
    . . . by C.I. Scofield
    These brief studies provide an essential framework for understanding how various parts of the Bible fit together harmoniously and purposefully. The study includes a helpful chart of The Seven Dispensations.
  • "Bible Study Outlines"
    . . . by C.I. Scofield
    Eighty-one brief lessons, divided into four volumes (with advancing levels of understanding), provide amazing clarity on major biblical topics, through the simple comparison of scripture with scripture.
  • "Old Testament Reflections of Christ"
    . . . by Paul R. Van Gorder
    These studies provide an overview of each of the Old Testament books, and are utilized for the Old Testament book 'sketches' (above).
  • "Christ in All the Scriptures"
    . . . by A.M. Hodgkin
    These studies provide an overview of all sixty-six books in the Bible. The New Testament studies are utilized for the NT book 'sketches' (above). The Old Testament studies provide additional insights to the similar studies by Paul Van Gorder (above).
  • Bible Book Notes
    Compared to the two studies immediately above, the Book Notes provide a more in depth, verse by verse, look at each book. Notes are included for several books. More will be added as opportunity allows. (For easy access, use the Book Notes button on the button bar.)
  • "Christ in the Tabernacle"
    . . . by W.W. Rugh
    This valuable study about the pictures of Christ's Person and Work, as seen in the design and worship of the Tabernacle, can also be accessed via the Book Notes on Exodus.
