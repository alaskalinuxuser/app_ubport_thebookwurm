The Book

Use the Buttons (on the button bar above this window) to access 'the Book' and available resources.
[NOTE: In the default viewing mode, there will also be two smaller windows below the window that you are now reading. If not, see 'Frames Help' on the Help Menu, to find viewing options appropriate for your device.]
Button functions:
  • Home - 3 Windows or Home - 3 Columns- Either button will return 'The Book' to the opening page configuration. As noted in the 'Frames Help' article (see link above), there are two multi-window viewing modes. Throughout this publication, all links to 'The Book' return to the default '3 Window' mode. Use the 'Home' buttons to switch to the mode that works best on your device.
  • Resource Menu- Displays a Menu of lessons and resources in the lower left window. Selected resources usually display in the upper window. In some cases, a menu will open in the lower left window with a Table of Contents for the selected resource.
  • Book Notes- Displays a Menu of available studies on books of the Bible (in the lower left window). When a book is selected for study, an outline of that book displays in the lower left window. Notes on a selected section will display in the upper window. The many scripture reference links within the study notes will display in the lower windows. Usually the Bible chapter under study appears in the lower left window.
  • Bible Text- Displays an index of the Bible text arranged in the order the books appear in most printed Bibles. The selected chapter appears in the lower right window. At the end of every chapter, a link allows the reader to progress to the next chapter.
  • Glossary- Accesses 'The King James Bible Companion' which provides definitions of more than 500 archaic words used in the KJV Bible. This resource opens in a separate browser window, to facilitate access, without interrupting your session in 'The Book'. During a given session, use this link (or the Glossary button) the first time you need this resource. Then, simply move between windows to view either 'The Book' or 'The King James Bible Companion' as needed.
  • Help Menu- Provides guidance for best display and printing of 'The Book' resources. Provides author contact and copyright information for this publication.
