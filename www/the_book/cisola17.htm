Sin, Part III

The Sin Question

  1. What is sin?
    • 1John 3:4 - "Sin is the transgression of the law"
    • Romans 14:23 - "Whatsoever is not of faith is sin"
    • 1John 5:17 - "All unrighteousness is sin"
  2. Are all sinners?
    • Romans 3:9 - "All under sin"
    • Romans 3:12 - "All gone out of the way"
    • Romans 3:19 - "Guilty before God." We may think well of ourselves, or people may think well of us -- this is what God thinks.
    • Romans 3:23 - "For all have sinned"
  3. The effect of sin
    • Ezekiel 18:4 - "Shall die"
    • Ecclesiastes 9:18 - "Destroyeth much good"
    • James 1:15 - "Bringeth forth death"
    • John 3:20 - "Hateth the light"
    • Ephesians 2:5 - "Dead in sins"
  4. The unbeliever's sins
    • John 8:24 - "Shall die in your sins"
    • 1Timothy 5:24 - "Sins... follow after"
    • John 15:22 - "No cloak for their sin"
    • John 9:41 - "Your sin remaineth"
  5. The believer's sins
    1. Where they were
      • Isaiah 53:6 - "Hath laid on Him"
      • 2Corinthians 5:21 - "Hath made Him to be sin"
      • 1Peter 2:24 - "His own self bare our sins"
      • Hebrews 1:3 - "By Himself purged our sins"
    2. Where they are
      • Isaiah 43:25 - "Blotteth out... will not remember"
      • Isaiah 44:22 - "Blotted out"
      • Psalm 103:12 - "As far as the east is from the west... removed"
      • Isaiah 38:17 - "Cast... behind thy back"
      • Micah 7:19 - "Into the depths of the sea"
      • Colossians 2:13 - "Forgiven"
      • Hebrews 10:17 - "Remember no more"
