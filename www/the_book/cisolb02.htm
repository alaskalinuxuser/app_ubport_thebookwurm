Christ's Teaching About Salvation

  1. The condition of men without Christ
    • Luke 19:10 - "Lost"
    • John 3:16 - "Perish"
    • John 3:18 - "Condemned"
    • John 3:19 - "Their deeds were evil"
    • John 3:20 - "Every one that doeth evil hateth the light"
    • John 3:36 - "Wrath of God abideth on him"
    • John 8:44 - "Ye are of your father the devil"
    • Mark 7:21-23 - "All these evil things come from within"
  2. The sacrificial death of Christ is necessary for the salvation of men
    • Matthew 26:27,28 - "Blood... which is shed for many for the remission of sins"
    • Hebrews 9:22 - "Without shedding of blood is no remission." The word translated "remission" is elsewhere rendered "forgiveness" (Mark 3:29), "deliverance" (Luke 4:18), and "at liberty" (Luke 4:18).
    • Matthew 20:28 - "Give His life a ransom [purchase price]." The same word is also translated "redeemed" in 1Peter 1:18,19
    • John 10:11 - "Giveth His life for the sheep" (see also John 10:15)
    • John 12:24-33 - "If I be lifted up from the earth, will draw all men unto Me" (see also John 3:14,15)
    • John 19:30 - "It is finished"
  3. Christ's complete salvation includes
    1. Forgiveness
      • Luke 7:48 - "Thy sins are forgiven"
      • Ephesians 1:7 - "We have... forgiveness of sins"
    2. Regeneration (new nature)
      • John 3:5-7 - "Ye must be born again"
      • Matthew 7:17,18 - "Every good tree bringeth forth good fruit"
    3. Eternal life (new vitality)
      • John 10:10 - "That they might have life"
    4. Sonship (new relation)
      • John 20:17 - "I ascend to My Father, and your Father"
    5. Keeping
      • John 10:28,29 - "Neither shall any man pluck them out of My hand"
      • Luke 22:31,32 - "Satan hath desired to have you... but I have prayed for thee"
      • John 17:11 - "Keep... those whom thou hast given Me"
      • John 6:39 - "Of all which He hath given Me... lose nothing"
    6. The gift of the Holy Spirit
      • John 14:17 - "For He dwelleth with you, and shall be with you"
      • 1Corinthians 6:19 - "Your body is the temple of the Holy Ghost which is in you"
    7. Glory
      • John 14:2 - "I go to prepare a place for you"
      • John 17:24 - "I will that they... may behold My glory"
  4. Salvation is believing in Christ
    • Luke 7:50 - "Thy faith hath saved thee" (see also Luke 8:48)
    • John 3:16 - "Whosoever believeth in Him"
    • John 3:36 - "Believeth on the Son hath everlasting life" (see also John 5:24; 6:47)
