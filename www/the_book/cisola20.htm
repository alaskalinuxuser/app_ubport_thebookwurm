by C.I. Scofield

Christ, Our Deliverer

"Who delivered us from so great a death, and doth deliver: in whom we trust that He will yet deliver us" (2Corinthians 1:10).
  1. Christ delivers us from the penalty of sin by the sacrifice of Himself
    • Colossians 1:13 - "Delivered us from the power of darkness"
    • 1Thessalonians 1:10 - "Delivered us from the wrath to come"
    • Ephesians 1:7 - "Redemption through His blood"
    • Colossians 2:13 - "Forgiven you all trespasses"
    • John 5:24 - "Shall not come into condemnation [judgment]"
    • Matthew 1:21 - "Save His people from their sins"
  2. Christ delivers us from the power of sin by His daily care
    • 2Timothy 3:11 - "The Lord delivered me"
    • 2Timothy 4:17,18 - "I was delivered"
    • 2Peter 2:9 - "Knoweth how to deliver"
    • Hebrews 7:25 - "Able also to save"
    • Psalm 23:1-6 - "Thou art with me"
    • Philippians 2:12,13 - "God worketh in you"
  3. Christ will deliver us from the presence of sin at His second coming, at which time He will come to earth personally and bodily
    • John 14:1-3 - "I will come again"
    • Hebrews 9:28 - "Shall He appear"
    • Philippians 3:20,21 - "Who shall change our vile body"
    • 1Thessalonians 4:16,17 - "We... shall be caught up"
    • John 17:22 - "Glory... given them"
    • Romans 8:18,19 - "Glory which shall be revealed in us"
    • Titus 2:13,14 - "Redeem us from all iniquity"
