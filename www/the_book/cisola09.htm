Volume 1, Lesson 9
by C.I. Scofield

The Holy Spirit, Part V

The Work of the Spirit

  1. Toward the world
    • John 14:17 - "Whom the world cannot receive"
    • John 16:8,9 - "Reprove [convict] the world of sin... because they believe not on Me"
  2. In the believer
    1. When the sinner believes on Christ he is born of the Spirit
      • John 3:5,6 - "Born of the Spirit"
      • Galatians 4:29 - "Born according to the Spirit"
    2. The Holy Spirit helps the believer understand God's Word
      • John 14:26 - "The Holy Ghost... shall teach you all things, and bring all things to your remembrance, whatsoever I have said unto you"
      • John 16:13-15 - "When He, the Spirit of truth, is come, He will guide you into all truth"
      • 1Corinthians 2:12-14 - "The Holy Ghost teacheth"
    3. Through the Spirit the believer has victory over self
      • Romans 8:2 - "Spirit of life... hath made me free"
      • Romans 8:13 - "Through the Spirit... mortify the deeds of the body"
      • Galatians 5:16 - "Walk in the Spirit, and ye shall not fulfil the lust of the flesh"
    4. The Holy Spirit aids believers
      • Galatians 4:6 - "Spirit... crying, Abba, Father" (see also Romans 8:15)
      • Romans 8:26 - "The Spirit itself maketh intercession for us"
    5. The Holy Spirit gives true Christian experience
      • Romans 14:17 - "Righteousness, and peace, and joy in the Holy Ghost"
      • Romans 8:5,6 - "To be spiritualy minded is life and peace"
      • Romans 15:13 - "Hope, through... the power of the Holy Ghost"
      • Galatians 5:22,23 - "Fruit of the Spirit"
    6. In service, the Holy Spirit:
      • Acts 1:8 - "Shall receive power." He fills the believer for power.
      • Acts 16:7,9 - "Spirit suffered them not." He guides the believer.
      • 1Corinthians 12:4-7 - "Given by the Spirit." He bestows gifts on the believer.
