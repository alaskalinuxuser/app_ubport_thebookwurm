Volume 4, Lesson 8
by C.I. Scofield

The Chosen People Redeemed, Part II

Preparation of a Deliverer (Exodus 2-4)

  1. Moses, a picture of Christ
    1. Moses was a divinely chosen deliverer
      • Exodus 3:1,2 -- "Moses... and the angel of the Lord appeared unto him"
      • Exodus 3:9,10 -- "Come now, therefore, and I will send thee"
    2. Rejected by "his own," Moses goes to Midian
      • Exodus 2:11-15 -- "Who made thee a prince and a judge over us?"
    3. During Moses' rejection by "his own," he gains a Gentile bride
      • Exodus 2:16-21 -- "And he gave Moses Zipporah"
    4. Afterward Moses again appears as Israel's deliverer and is accepted
      • Exodus 4:29-31 -- "And the people believed"
  2. Christ the Redeemer
    1. Christ is our Deliverer
      • John 3:16 -- "For God... gave His only begotten Son"
      • John 6:57 -- "As the living Father hath sent Me"
      • John 6:29 -- "Him whom He hath sent."
        (See also Isaiah 61:1; Luke 4:18; 2Corinthians 1:10; 1Thessalonians 1:10)
    2. Christ, rejected by the Jews, turned to the Gentiles
      • John 1:11 -- "His own received Him not"
      • Luke 20:2 -- "By what authority?"
      • Mark 16:15 -- "Go ye into all the world"
      • Acts 28:25-28 -- "The heart of this people is waxed gross... the salvation of God is sent unto the Gentiles"
    3. Christ's bride is the Church
      • Matthew 12:14-21 -- "And in His name shall the Gentiles trust"
      • 2Corinthians 11:2 -- "Espoused... to Christ"
      • Ephesians 5:30-33 -- "I speak concerning Christ and the Church"
    4. Christ will return again and be accepted
      • Acts 15:14-17 -- "After this I will return"
      • Zechariah 12:10 -- "They shall look upon Me whom they have pierced"
      • Romans 11:24-26 -- "All Israel... saved"
  3. Moses typifies Christ as:
    1. Prophet
      • Acts 3:22,23 -- "A prophet... like unto me"
    2. Advocate
      • Exodus 32:31-35 -- "Moses returned unto the Lord... forgive their sin"
      • 1John 2:1,2 -- "We have an advocate... Jesus Christ"
    3. Intercessor
      • Exodus 17:1-6 -- "Moses cried unto the Lord"
      • Hebrews 7:25 -- "He ever liveth to make intercession for them"
    4. Leader
      • Deuteronomy 33:4,5 -- "Moses commanded"
      • Isaiah 55:4 -- "I have given Him for a... leader"
      • Hebrews 2:10 -- "Captain of their salvation"

      Moses and Christ were both "faithful," but Moses was a faithful servant, while Chirst was a faithful Son (see Hebrews 3:5,6).
