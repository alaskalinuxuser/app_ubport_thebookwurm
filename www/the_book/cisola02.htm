The Fatherhood of God

  1. God is the Father of the Lord Jesus Christ
    1. The eternal relationship
      • John 1:1-3 - "In the beginning... was God... with God... all things were made by Him"
      • John 17:5 - "O Father... glory which I had with Thee before the world was"
      • John 17:21 - "As Thou, Father, art in Me, and I in Thee"
    2. The relationship in incarnation
      • Luke 1:31,32 - "Son of the Highest"
      • Luke 1:35 - "Power of the Highest... called the Son of God"
      • John 1:14 - "The only begotten of the Father"
      • John 8:42 - "I proceeded forth and came from God"
      • John 20:17 - "I ascend unto My Father, and your Father"
  2. God is the Father of all who receive Jesus by faith
    • John 1:12 - "As many as received Him... become... sons [children]"
    • John 1:13 - "Which were born... of God"
    • John 3:6 - "Born of the Spirit"
    • Galatians 3:26 - "Children... by faith in Christ Jesus"
    • 1John 5:1 - "Whosoever believeth... is born of God"
    • John 20:17 - "My Father and your Father"
  3. Our Fellowship is with the Father and Son
    Fellowship is having something in common with another. The Father has given us His eternal Son, and the Son has brought us to the Father.
    • 1John 1:3 - "Our fellowship is with the Father"
    • 1Corinthians 8:6 - "To us... the Father"
  4. We Worship the Father
    • John 4:23 - "True worshippers shall worship the Father"
    • 2Corinthians 1:3 - "Blessed be God, even the Father"
    • Ephesians 1:3 - "Blessed be God and the Father"
  5. We bring our needs to the Father
    • Matthew 6:26 - "Your... Father feedeth... better than they"
    • Matthew 6:32 - "Knoweth that ye have need"
    • Matthew 7:9-11 - "Father... give... to them that ask"
