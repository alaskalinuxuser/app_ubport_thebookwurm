Volume 2, Lesson 1
by C.I. Scofield

The Love of God

  1. God's love is without measure
    • Ephesians 3:18,19 - "To comprehend... the breadth, and length, and depth, and height; and to know the love of Christ"
    • John 3:15,16 - "Whosoever believeth in Him"
    • Jeremiah 31:3 - "Loved thee with an everlasting love"
    • Galatians 2:20 - "The Son of God, who loved me"
    • Ephesians 2:4 - "His great love"
    • John 3:16 - "For God so loved the world, that He gave." It has been said that the most expressive word in any language is the English word so in John 3:16.
  2. Who God loves
    • John 3:16 - "God so loved the world"
    • Ephesians 2:4,5 - "He loved us, even when we were dead in sins"
    • Galatians 2:20 - "Who loved me"
  3. How God loves
    • Hosea 14:4 - "I will love them freely"
    • John 16:27 - "The Father Himself loveth you." He loves as a Father.
    • John 13:1 - "He loved them unto the end"
    • John 17:23 - "Hast loved them, as Thou hast loved Me." God loves us as He loves His Son.
  4. What God's love has done for us
    • 1John 4:10 - "Sent His Son to be the propitiation for our sins." Jesus died on the cross for our sins as an atoning sacrifice.
    • Revelation 1:5 - "Washed us from our sins"
    • Titus 3:4,5 - "He saved us"
    • John 3:16 - "Not perish, but have everlasting life"
    • Ephesians 2:6 - "Raised us up together, and made us sit together in heavenly places"
    • 2Thessalonians 2:16 - "Hath given us everlasting consolation and good hope"
  5. What God's love will do for us
    • Romans 8:37 - "We are more than conquerors"
    • Romans 8:38,39 - "[Nothing]... shall be able to separate us from the love of God"
    • Hebrews 12:6 - "Whom the Lord loveth He chasteneth [disciplines]"
