Bible Study Outlines

Volume 2, Lesson 15
by C.I. Scofield

Christian Service

  1. We are called to serve the Lord
    • John 2:5 - "Whatsoever He saith unto you, do it"
    • John 15:14 - "If ye do whatsoever I command you"
    • Matthew 23:10 - "One is your Master, even Christ"
    • Luke 16:13 - "No servant can serve two masters"
  2. The servant's place and work are sovereignly appointed
    • Romans 12:4 - "All... have not the same office"
    • Romans 12:6 - "Having then gifts differing"
    • 1Corinthians 12:4-11 - "Diversities of gifts... dividing... as He will"
    • 1Corinthians 12:18 - "Set the members... as it hath pleased Him"
    • Galatians 1:1 - "An apostle, (not of men, neither by man, but by Jesus Christ)"
  3. Every believer has a definite spiritual gift for service
    • 1Corinthians 12:7 - "Given to every man"
    • Mark 13:34 - "To every man his work"
    • 1Corinthians 12:14-16 - "The body is not one member, but many"
    • 1Corinthians 12:28-30 - "God hath set"
  4. All believers are to be witnesses
    • Acts 1:8 - "Ye shall be witnesses." A witness must testify to facts within his own knowledge (see also John 9:11).
    • John 3:11 - "We speak that we do know"
    • 2Timothy 1:12 - "I know whom I have believed"
  5. The conditions of fruitful service
    1. Serve in the power of the Spirit
      • Acts 1:8 - "Power... the Holy Ghost is come upon you"
      • Ephesians 4:30 - "Grieve not the Holy Spirit"
      • Ephesians 4:31 - "Let all bitterness, and wrath, and anger... be put away from you"
      • 1Thessalonians 5:19 - "Quench not the Spirit"
    2. Serve while abiding (continuing in obedience)
      • John 15:10 - "Abide in My love"
      • John 15:5 - "He that abideth in Me... bringeth forth much fruit"
      • John 15:7 - "If ye abide in Me... ask what ye will... done unto you"
  6. The place of service
    • Acts 1:8 - "Unto the uttermost part of the earth"
    • Luke 24:47 - "Among all nations"
    • Matthew 13:38 - "The field is the world"
    • Mark 16:15 - "Every creature"
