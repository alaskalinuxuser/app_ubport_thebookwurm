Matthew

Book Outline & Menu of Notes

Key & Guide to Notes
- Genealogy & Birth of Jesus- Mat 1:1-25
- Infancy of Jesus- Mat 2:1-23
- Ministry of John the Baptist- Mat 3:1-17
- Temptation & Early Ministry of Jesus- 4:1-25
- The Sermon on the Mount-
( Principles of the Kingdom )
-- True Blessedness, Mat 5:1-16
-- True Righteousness, 5:17-48
-- True Religion, Mat 6:1-18
-- True Riches, 6:19-34
-- Testing the True, Mat 7:1-29
- Demonstration of the King's Power - over...
--disease, nature, the supernatural, 8:1-34
--issues of: guilt, time, death, Mat 9:1-38
- Twelve Disciples called, commissioned & prepared
to preach the Gospel of the Kingdom- 10:1-42
- Foreshadows of the King's rejection-
--unreceptivity of the people, 11:1-30
--opposition of the leaders, 12:1-50
- Seven Parables, Mat 13:1-58
(The Mysteries of the Kingdom of Heaven)
- The Ministry of the Rejected King-
  • Uncompromised Authority,
    in a climate of injustice- 14:1-36
  • The Truth,
    in a time of hypocrisy- 15:1-39
  • The Rock,
    amidst man's uncertainty - 16:1-28
    (At this point, the King stops preaching the Gospel of the Kingdom. He begins to speak of His 'church', and of His death & resurrection.)
  • Heaven's Glory (the beloved Son),
    amidst human faithlessness, 17:1-27
- The King teaches His followers concerning...
-- Their Relationships. Mat 18:1-35
-- Their Way of Life. Mat 19:1-30
-- Their Reward. Mat 20:1-34
- The King presents
Himself to the Nation- 21:1-46
- The King confronts
the rulers of Israel- 22:1-46
- The King denounces the rulers,
and announces woe- Mat 23:1-39
- The King foretells great Tribulation,
prior to His Return- Mat 24:1-51
- The King warns us to be Watching
for His Coming- Mat 25:1-46
- The King offers Himself for His people-
- - Preparation of the Passover- 26:1-75
- - His sacrificial Death- Mat 27:1-66
- - His Resurrection- Mat 28:1-20
