Book Outline & Menu of Notes

Key & Guide to Notes
The Prophecy of Habakkuk -
- The Goodness of God and the Problem of Sin -
 
I. Habakkuk's Perplexity 1:1- 2:20
A. Introduction, Hab 1:1
B. Problem #1
  • Question:
    Why does God overlook the wickedness of Judah? 1:2-4
  • Answer:
    God's Plan: Judah will fall to Babylon. 1:5-11
C. Problem #2 (1:12- 2:20)
  • Question:
    Why will God use a more wicked nation as His instrument of judgment? 1:12- 2:1
  • Answer:
    God's Purpose: Hab 2:2-20
    1. Its Importance: Write... 2:2,3
    2. Its Principle: The Just shall Live by Faith. 2:4
    3. Its Execution: Woes upon the wicked. 2:5-19
    4. Its Perfection: Silence before Him 2:20
II. Habakkuk's Prayer to the LORD Hab 3:1-19
A. Requests to His Person, 3:1,2
  • Revive Thy Work.
  • Remember Mercy.
B. Remembrance of His Power...
  • in Israel's Deliverance from Egypt, 3:3-8
  • in Israel's Conquest of Canaan, 3:9-12
C. Recognition of His Purpose...
  • the Salvation of His People, 3:13-15
D. Reverance at His Predominance, 3:16
E. Rest in His Peace, 3:17-19
