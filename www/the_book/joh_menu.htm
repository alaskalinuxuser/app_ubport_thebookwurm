John

Book Outline & Menu of Notes

Key & Guide to Notes
A. The Word of God came (from eternity)-
John 1:1-18
B. His earthly ministry (3. 5 years)-
John 1:19-51 - -
- Announced by John:
Behold, the Lamb of God.
- Sought by disciples --
Come and see.
John 2:1-25 - -
- The first miracle --
My hour has not yet come.
- The first passover --
My Father's house...
John 3:1-36 - -
-To Nicodemus --
Ye must be born again.
-John the Baptist's last testimony:
He must increase...
John 4:1-54 - -
-The Samaritan woman --
The water I shall give...
-The nobleman's son --
Your son liveth.
John 5:1-47 - -
-The man at Bethesda --
Take up thy bed & walk.
-The sabbath Test --
My Father works and I work.
John 6:1-71 - -
-The feeding of 5000 --
I am the bread of life.
John 7:1-8:1 - -
-The feast of Tabernacles --
If any man thirst, let him come to me.
John 8:1-59 - -
-The adulteress & the Pharisees--
I am the light of the world.
-The Light from God & men in darkness--
Before Abraham was, I am.
John 9:1-41 - -
-The man born blind & the blind Pharisees--
Ye say, 'We see.' ... Your sin remains.
John 10:1-42 - -
-Parables about shepherds and sheep-
- My sheep hear my voice...
- I am the Door of the sheep...
- I am the Good Shepherd...
John 11:1-57 - -
-The raising of Lazarus --
I am the resurrection and the Life.
C. His last week, public ministry -
John 12:1-50 - -
-The supper in Bethany, the Triumphal Entry, the gentiles seek Him -
The hour is come...
D. His last night, private ministry to His disciples -
John 13:1-38 - -
-Jesus washes the disciples' feet --
I have given you an example...
-At supper --
One of you shall betray me.
-To the disciples --
Where I go, ye cannot come...
-To Peter --
The cock shall not crow, till...
John 14:1-30 - -
-Let not your heart be troubled...
- If I go, I will come again...
- I am the Way, the Truth and the Life...
- If ye had known me... my Father also.
- I will not leave you comfortless...
- Peace I leave with you...
John 15:1-16:4 - -
- I am the vine, ye are the branches...
- I have chosen you...
- The world hates you {as it hates Me}.
John 16:4-33 - -
-He instructs them concerning --
- The work of the world: persecution.
- The work of the Holy Spirit:
     -- to convict the world.
     -- to guide believers.
     -- to speak of Christ.
- His departure:
     I came... I go to the Father.
- Their sorrow and subsequent joy.
John 17:1-26 - -
-His High Priestly prayer --
Father, the hour is come...
E. His hour -
John 18:1-40 - -
-His arrest and trials.
-Peter's denials.
John 19:1-42 - -
-His crucifixion - It is finished !
F. His resurrection and appearances -
John 20:1-31 - -
-Within a week, in Jerusalem -
Peace be unto you...
As my Father has sent me, so send I you.
Blessed are they... that believe.
John 21:1-25 - -
-Later, in Galilee -
If ye love me, feed my sheep.
