Book Outline & Menu of Notes

Key & Guide to Notes
- Zephaniah -
The Day of the LORD, and His Hidden Ones.
I. Introduction, Zeph 1:1
II. Judgment Nearing: Picture of the Day of the LORD, 1:2- 2:3
1. Judgment will consume the whole World, 1:2-3
2. Judgment upon Judah and Jerusalem, 1:4- 2:3
  • A call to hide in Him. 2:1-3
3. Judgment upon Surrounding Nations, 2:4-15
III. Judgment Ended: Work of the LORD "in the midst" 3:1-20
1. His Justice: shameless Jerusalem punished, 3:1-7
2. His Purging: the waiting Remnant purified, 3:8-13
3. His Rest: with His hidden Treasure rejoicing, 3:14-20
