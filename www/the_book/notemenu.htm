Notes on Books of the Bible

Table of Contents

  • Genesis -
    The book of beginnings.
  • Exodus -
    The separation of Israel.
  • Ruth -
    A love story leading to Christ.
  • Psalms of the Messiah -
    Prophetic portraits of Christ.
  • Proverbs of Solomon -
    Wisdom, in the Person of Christ.
  • Isaiah -
    Warnings of judgment, Promises of salvation, to Israel.
  • Jeremiah -
    More warnings and promises, delivered with tears.
  • Lamentations -
    Israel's tears, following disregarded warnings.
  • Daniel -
    The calendar of Gentile world dominion.
  • Hosea -
    The LORD's undying Love for unfaithful Israel.
  • Joel -
    The Day of the LORD.
  • Amos -
    The Certainty of Judgment upon Israel (and the nations).
  • Obadiah -
    The Judgment of Edom for their Hatred of Israel.
  • Jonah -
    The God of Israel is God of the Gentiles, also.
  • Micah -
    Who is Like the LORD?
  • Nahum -
    The Judgment of Nineveh is a Comfort to Israel.
  • Habakkuk -
    The Goodness of God and the Problem of Sin.
  • Zephaniah -
    The Day of the LORD, and His Hidden Ones.
  • Haggai -
    The Completion of the LORD's House.
  • Zechariah -
    The Certainty of Israel's Restoration.
  • Malachi -
    The Messenger of the LORD.
  • Matthew -
    The Life & Work of Jesus Christ.
  • John -
    The Life & Work of Jesus Christ (another perspective).
  • Acts -
    The risen Christ begins to build His Church.
  • Romans -
    The Gospel of Christ explained to all.
  • Ephesians -
    The Church of Christ: The Fullness of His Body.
  • Philippians -
    The Church of Christ: The Fullness of Joy in Selfless Fellowship.
  • Colossians -
    The Church of Christ: The Fullness of its Head.
  • 1Timothy -
    The Local Church: Governed with Godly Teaching.
  • 2Timothy -
    The Man of God: Prepared for the Master's Use.
  • Titus -
    The Local Church: Governed for Godly Living.
  • Hebrews -
    The superiority of Christ over all.
  • 1 & 2 Peter -
    On living for Christ in difficult times.
  • Revelation -
    Things that must shortly come to pass.
