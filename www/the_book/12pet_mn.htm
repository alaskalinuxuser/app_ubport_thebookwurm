1 & 2 Peter

Book Outline & Menu of Notes

Key & Guide to Notes
1PETER -
The Christian's Hope, in a time of Trial.
1. Suffering & Security - Joy. 1Peter 1 (v.1-9)
2. Suffering & the Scriptures - Holiness (v.10-25)
3. Suffering & the Suffering of Christ-
a. Separation - 1Peter 2
b. Conduct - 1Peter 3
c. Obedience - 1Peter 4
4. Suffering & the Second Coming of Christ-
a. Service & Hope - 1Peter 5 (v.1-4)
b. Humility & Patience (v.5-14)
2PETER -
The Christian's Knowledge of Christ,
in a time of Apostasy.
1. ADDITION of Christian Graces
Gives Assurance. 2Peter 1 (v.1-14)
2. AUTHORITY of the Scriptures
Attested by Fulfilled Prophecy (v.15-21)
3. APOSTASY-
Brought in by False Teachers. 2Peter 2
4. ATTITUDE Toward Return of the Lord:
- a Test of Apostates. 2Peter 3 (v.1-4)
5. AGENDA of God for the World (v.5-13) -
a. Past world (v.5,6)
b. Present world (v.7-12)
c. Future world (v.13)
6. ADMONITION to Believers (v.14-18)
